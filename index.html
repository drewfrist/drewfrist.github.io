About Me

I'm a graphic designer, living in West Los Angeles. I became interested in Front-End Web Design and Development because of the increasing need for designers to know how to develop, customize, and improve clients’ web-based projects and, at the very least, speak to their developers in a common language.

My Mission

As I learn more about front-end, I hope to one day work as a print designer with his own online print store because I like to work in a self-fueled work environment that fosters limitless creative play.
